A Present From Paradise

A shooting star has bestowed,
I will bring you the rainbow,
As I would fight with demons below,
And will bear your pain through and through.

A present from paradise,
I adore and you mesmerize,
Eyes that glow in dark,
I found you as my constant lighting spark.

A cheerful odyssey,
Has engaged witches with courtesy,
Leaning forward to have that bliss,
Just be there, I left with a forehead kiss.

I am the fire,
You are the rain,
It's a mistake to be imagined together,
Now the downpour has turned me into ash forever.

Poem By::

Mayank Kanware
