Sometimes I Feel

Sometimes,
I don't want,
I don't want to pick my pen up,
And, start reciting everything to my diary,
She knows that I am afraid and lost.

I don't want to remain on ground,
Knowing I just lost my wings,
And, I am flattered being not,
So, I end up picking up my pen.

I don't even know the place,
I am fond of,
I used to visit,
Sit and watch the sky lie to stars,
Just, because I know
I am lost.
I just wanted to write poetries,
That have metaphors and rhymes,
And the truth based on my life,
But, I find sadness bundled up at the back of my diary.

So to tell you the harsh truth
Life is a moving train
And I want to hurl my body
Out the door.
Yes yes yes
I am okay!
Because I understand
The only way out
Is through.

Poem By::

Mayank Kanware
