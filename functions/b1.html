Why We Should Read Books?

An average CEO reads about 60 books a month. almost every rich & successful people read books to increase their knowledge & Wisdom. BILL GATES reads about one book a week despite being so rich. WARREN BUFFET (World's NO'1 investor)used to read around 600 to 1k pages per day during the starting of his career. Elon Musk (founder of $3 billion company), Mark Cuban, Oprah Winfrey, Mark Zuckerburg & all billionaire have a common habit of reading. These all great people despite being so rich and successful still keep reading books because they all know that the more they "learn" the more they "earn".It's not about just earning, reading also increases focus, concentration & intelligence. Many researches have also shown that reading books reduces stress, which ultimately results in a happier life. That's the reason that the top 1% of the world always keep reading & learning from books.
